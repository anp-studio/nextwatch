# TMDB Attribution

> **This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.**

<img src="/images/tmdb-logo.svg" alt="TMDB" class="mt-4 h-8 w-auto" />


## About TMDB

NextWatch uses data provided by **The Movie Database (TMDB)** to display movie information throughout the application.

Depending on the feature, this may include:

* Movie titles
* Posters and backdrops
* Overviews
* Release dates
* Genres
* Runtime
* Ratings
* Cast and crew
* Trailers
* Other related metadata

This information helps provide a better movie discovery experience while keeping the application lightweight and up to date.

## Ownership

All TMDB-provided content remains the property of its respective owners.

NextWatch does not claim ownership of any movie metadata, images, trailers, or other content obtained through the TMDB API.
