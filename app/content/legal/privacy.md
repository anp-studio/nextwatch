## Overview

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information NextWatch collects, how it is used, and the choices you have regarding your data.

NextWatch is an open-source movie recommendation application built for the Youth Hacking 4 Freedom 2026 competition.

## Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the application, we may collect:

### Account Information

When you create an account, we may store:

* Your email address
* Your authentication provider (such as Google)
* Basic profile information provided by your authentication provider

Authentication is securely handled by Supabase. NextWatch does not store your password directly.

### Movie Activity

To provide personalized recommendations and synchronize your account, we store information such as:

* Your watch history
* Your watchlist
* Movie ratings
* Recommendation preferences
* AI-generated recommendation history where applicable

### Technical Information

To operate and protect the service, we may temporarily process technical information including:

* IP address
* Browser and device information
* Request logs
* Error reports
* Rate limiting and abuse prevention data

This information is used only for security, diagnostics, and improving the reliability of the application.

## How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

* Authenticate your account
* Synchronize your library across devices
* Generate personalized movie recommendations
* Improve application performance
* Detect abuse and protect the service
* Respond to support requests when necessary

We do **not** sell your personal information.

## AI Features

NextWatch may use third-party AI providers to generate personalized movie recommendations.

Only the information necessary to generate recommendations is shared with the selected provider. This may include parts of your watch history, watchlist, ratings, and preference data.

## Third-Party Services

NextWatch currently relies on several third-party services, including:

* Supabase for authentication and database services
* Google OAuth for optional sign-in
* TMDB for movie information
* AI providers such as Google Gemini and OpenAI for recommendation generation
* Vercel for hosting
* Cloudflare for DNS and security
* Resend for transactional emails

Each provider processes information according to its own privacy policy.

## Cookies

NextWatch uses essential authentication cookies and session tokens required to sign in and keep you logged in.

The application does not intentionally use advertising or tracking cookies.

## Data Retention

Your account data is stored until you delete your account or request its removal.

Some server logs may be retained for a limited time to help detect abuse, investigate issues, and maintain service reliability.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

* Access your personal information
* Correct inaccurate information
* Delete your account and associated data
* Request a copy of your stored data

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as NextWatch evolves.

Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the NextWatch team through the project's GitHub repository.
